# About 
Dailyexpense is a Frontend Web Application that helps everyone to enter his payments or incomes everyday, and after that he can observe his expenses, and sees some reports and charts, it's simple, at start you just need to register and define your categories like: food, transport, salary, bills, others....., and after that you can enter your expense , select the date and category and how much you pay or have income
